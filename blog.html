TheGrandChess9: A Powerful New Evolution Redefining the Future of Chess

Published: 20 February 2026

A groundbreaking innovation in the world of strategy gaming has arrived with the launch of TheGrandChess9. This bold and visionary evolution of classical chess expands traditional boundaries and introduces revolutionary elements designed to challenge both beginners and experienced players alike.

Expanding the Battlefield: 9×9 Board

Unlike the traditional 8×8 chessboard, TheGrandChess9 is played on a 9×9 board, creating a larger battlefield that opens the door to deeper strategy, expanded tactical combinations, and more dynamic gameplay.

Special Setup Phase Adds Strategic Depth

Before the official start of the match:

  • White places two extra pawns anywhere on Rank 3.
  • Black places two extra pawns anywhere on Rank 7.

Once both players complete this placement, the game officially begins, with White always making the first move. This innovative feature allows players to customize their early strategy and create countless opening variations.

Introducing the Divine Guardian: Charioteer (Sārthi)

One of the most distinctive features of TheGrandChess9 is the introduction of a new piece called the Charioteer (Sārthi), acting as the divine guardian and personal protector of the King.

The Charioteer must always remain adjacent to its King. If the King moves away, the player must make an additional compulsory move to reposition the Charioteer next to the King. During this special move, the Charioteer may be placed on any empty square surrounding the King — even if this action helps the King escape check.

  • The Charioteer cannot capture any piece.
  • It cannot be captured by any piece.

Its sole purpose is protection and companionship, introducing an entirely new defensive dynamic.

The Sceptre: The Ultimate Power Piece

The Sceptre is the most powerful attacking piece in TheGrandChess9. It combines the movement abilities of:

  • A Rook
  • A Bishop
  • A Knight

By merging all three movement patterns without restriction, the Sceptre dramatically increases attacking possibilities and tactical complexity.

Renamed Pieces and Unique Restrictions

  • The Rook is renamed Airavat.
  • The Queen is renamed Commander.
  • Castling is not permitted.

A Vision Beyond the Traditional Game

The creators describe TheGrandChess9 not merely as a variation, but as a strategic movement aimed at redefining how chess is played in the modern era. With tournaments, ranking systems, and competitive opportunities in development, the platform aspires to build a global community.

As chess enthusiasts worldwide continue to explore new dimensions of strategy, TheGrandChess9 invites players to step into the future of the game — where creativity, protection, power, and expanded tactics reshape the battlefield.
